Frequently Asked Questions

Pro subscriptions are managed through Apple. To cancel:

  • Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  • Tap your name at the top
  • Tap Subscriptions
  • Select AccessEd and tap Cancel Subscription

You'll keep Pro features until the end of your current billing period.

You can delete your account from within the app:

  • Go to Settings tab
  • Scroll to the Account section
  • Tap Delete Account
  • Confirm the deletion

This will permanently delete all your data including documents, quizzes, and learning progress. This action cannot be undone.

Free accounts include:

  • 10 documents total (delete documents to free up slots)
  • 20 AI questions per week
  • 5 quizzes per week
  • Documents limited to 30 pages each

Limits reset weekly. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited access.

Refunds for Pro subscriptions are handled by Apple. To request a refund:

  • Visit reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Sign in with your Apple ID
  • Find your AccessEd purchase and request a refund

If your document upload fails, try the following:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure the document is a PDF file
  • Free users: verify the document is under 30 pages
  • Make sure you haven't reached your document limit
  • Try uploading again - sometimes temporary server issues occur

If the problem persists, email us at support@accessed.app with details about the document and error message.
